Subject: Formal Appeal against Rejection dated {rejection_date} – {patient_name} – Insurance No.: {insurance_number}

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to formally appeal your decision dated {rejection_date} to decline coverage for outpatient psychotherapy with {private_therapist_name}.

My application included all required documents demonstrating that the conditions for reimbursement under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V are met. Under § 2 Abs. 1 SGB V, you are obligated to ensure I receive timely access to care — something I was unable to achieve through the standard route despite extensive documented efforts. My entitlement to reimbursement therefore stands.

I hope you will reconsider upon reviewing the documentation. Should the appeal be unsuccessful, I will have no choice but to pursue the matter through legal channels and notify the supervisory authority (Aufsichtsbehörde) as well as the Federal Patient Commissioner (Patientenbeauftragter der Bundesregierung).

Kind regards,
{patient_name}
Insurance number: {insurance_number}
