Subject: Formal Appeal against Rejection dated {rejection_date} – {patient_name} – Insurance No.: {insurance_number}

Dear Sir or Madam,

I hereby lodge a formal appeal (Widerspruch) against your letter dated {rejection_date}, in which you declined to cover the costs arising from outpatient psychotherapy with {private_therapist_name}. My application included all required documents demonstrating that the conditions for reimbursement are met. Under § 2 Abs. 1 SGB V, you are legally obligated to ensure I receive timely care. As you have failed to do so, my entitlement to reimbursement under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V stands.

I therefore ask you once again to approve my application. Should you not do so, I will pursue my claim through legal channels and notify the supervisory authority (Aufsichtsbehörde) as well as the Federal Patient Commissioner (Patientenbeauftragter der Bundesregierung).

Kind regards,
{patient_name}
Insurance number: {insurance_number}
