Subject: No Response: Cost Reimbursement Application dated {application_date} – {patient_name} – Insurance No.: {insurance_number}

Dear Sir or Madam,

On {application_date} I submitted an application for cost reimbursement under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V. On {followup_date} I sent a follow-up, as I had received no response. To date, I have still not heard from you.

Under § 2 Abs. 1 SGB V, you are legally obligated to ensure I receive timely care. Your failure to respond to my application under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V is incompatible with this obligation.

I hereby demand that you process my application without delay and provide me with a written decision. If I do not receive a response within two weeks, I will pursue my claim through legal channels and notify the supervisory authority (Aufsichtsbehörde) as well as the Federal Patient Commissioner (Patientenbeauftragter der Bundesregierung).

Kind regards,
{patient_name}
Insurance number: {insurance_number}
