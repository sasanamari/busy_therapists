Subject: Application for Cost Reimbursement under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V – {patient_name} – Insurance No.: {insurance_number}

Dear Sir or Madam,

I hereby request that you cover the costs arising from outpatient psychotherapy with {private_therapist_name} and confirm this in writing. {private_therapist_name} is an approved psychotherapist (approbierter Psychotherapeut/approbierte Psychotherapeutin) qualified in an approved therapy method (Richtlinienverfahren), but does not hold a licence with the statutory health insurance system.

Under § 2 Abs. 1 SGB V, statutory health insurers are required to ensure their members receive timely and adequate care. As the enclosed documents demonstrate, despite genuine efforts I have been unable to find a publicly funded therapist who can begin treating me within three months. I am therefore exercising my right under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V to obtain the necessary treatment myself and claim reimbursement of the costs.

As you can see from my attached contact list, I have made repeated unsuccessful attempts to find a publicly funded therapist (Kassenzulassung) who could treat me in a timely manner. My search showed that I would have to wait more than {max_wait_months} months for a first appointment. In contrast, it would be possible to begin treatment with {private_therapist_name} in the near future.

I am enclosing the following documents with this application:
- Contact list of publicly funded therapists approached
- PTV11 form with urgency note
- Confirmation from {private_therapist_name} of qualifications and availability
- Medical certificate of necessity / consultation report (Konsiliarbericht)

If you do not approve my application, please inform me as soon as possible of a publicly funded therapist near my place of residence who can offer me an appointment in the near future.

Kind regards,
{patient_name}
Insurance number: {insurance_number}
