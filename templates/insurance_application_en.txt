Subject: Application for Cost Reimbursement under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V – {patient_name} – Insurance No.: {insurance_number}

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to apply for cost reimbursement for outpatient psychotherapy under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V. Despite genuine efforts, I have been unable to find a publicly funded therapist who can begin treating me within three months. I am therefore exercising my right under § 13 Abs. 3 SGB V to obtain the necessary treatment myself, and I kindly ask you to cover the costs and confirm this in writing.

Under § 2 Abs. 1 SGB V, statutory health insurers are required to ensure their members receive timely and adequate care. As the enclosed contact list demonstrates, I made repeated unsuccessful attempts to find a publicly funded therapist available within that timeframe — my search showed I would have to wait more than three months for a first appointment. {private_therapist_name} is an approved psychotherapist (approbierter Psychotherapeut/approbierte Psychotherapeutin) who does not hold a licence with the statutory health insurance system, but is able to begin treatment in the near future.

I am enclosing the following documents with this application:
- Contact list of publicly funded therapists approached
- PTV11 form with urgency note
- Confirmation from {private_therapist_name} ({private_therapist_email}) of qualifications and availability
- Medical certificate of necessity / consultation report (Konsiliarbericht)

Kind regards,
{patient_name}
Insurance number: {insurance_number}
